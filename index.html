Raphael Hipolito

An illustrator and designer born and raised in the Republic of the Philippines. Grew up in a family who worked in fashion design industry, fine arts and Architecture. Moved in Canada in 2011 for family reasons. Aspired to be and Illustrator and Animator since childhood. My first introduction to Graphic Design was when I took multi media design in 2008 and learned basics in Adobe suites, Maya Autodesk and photography. In 2009-2011 I worked for a Civil engineer and a friend to do Autocad drawings of blue prints for his projects. I am Currently I am taking Graphic Design in Algonquin College to expand and learn more about design and art.
